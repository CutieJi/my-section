Rogue Vandals' Clan Rules


At RV:CL 2025, we believe that a set of guiding principles is essential to fostering a positive, productive, and fun environment for all members. Our clan rules are designed to promote:

- Teamwork & Collaboration: By working together, we can achieve greatness and overcome even the toughest challenges.
- Respect & Sportsmanship: We treat each other with respect, kindness, and empathy, both in victory and defeat.
- Growth & Improvement: We continuously learn, adapt, and improve our skills, strategies, and gameplay.
- Fun & Camaraderie: Most importantly, we have fun and enjoy each other's company, making gaming a positive and rewarding experience


By following our rules, you'll enjoy benefits like:

- Active Community: Engage with fellow members, participate in events, and stay updated on clan activities.
- Supportive Environment: Get tips, advice, and constructive feedback to help you improve your gameplay.
- Exciting Events: Participate in tournaments, challenges, and other activities that'll test your skills and reward your dedication.
- Lasting Friendships: Forge strong bonds with like-minded gamers who share your passion and enthusiasm.
